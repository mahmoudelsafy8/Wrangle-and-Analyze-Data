Insight 1: Highest Source Used¶

98.1% of users use iphones when they post dog images, it's a high percentage compared to the rest of the sources, I think it has something to do with the quality of the iphone device camera.

Insight 2: Most Popular Dog Type¶

The most of dog type common among the users from the top 5 list is Golden Retriever with 139 images, I think that's because he looks cute and small size more than Labrador Retriever and pembroke.

Insight 3: The Common Dog Stage¶

Pupper is the most common dog stage in WeRateDogs tweets, there is a high difference among the rest of dog stage.

Insight 4: Relation between the retweetes and Favorites "Like"¶

The archive data covers tweets from 2015-11 to 2017-8, it can be clearly the number of favorites "like" is not down warning and users tend to favorites 'like' more than retweets especially between 2016-06 till 2017-07, also Retweets amount is pretty stable as well but not like favorites for sure, maybe because most people tend to favorites 'like' as an easy and fast way while scrolling.
